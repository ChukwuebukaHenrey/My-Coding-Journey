Jake P. Fresco

Actor

ABOUT

Already performing in front of family and relatives at the age of three, Jake Fresco knew that performing is for him. With his deep passion for musical theater, he extended his theater geek fan status to performer status and starred in Performavery hit musicals, such as The Gray Paint as Cal and The Little Master as Jeffrey Stone.

Jake holds a Bachelor’s degree in Theater Studies from Hexton University. He has also won various acting awards like the Omond Award as Best Actor in a Musical for Jukebox Jester and the Lytton Awards Best Supporting Actor for the musical Romeo’s Juliet.

When he’s not performing, he enjoys staying home, and he volunteers with the Octagon Theater Charity as an acting coach to children and youths interested in theater.

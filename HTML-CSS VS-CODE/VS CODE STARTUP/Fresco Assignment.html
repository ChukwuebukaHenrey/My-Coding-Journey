Jake P. Fresco

Actor

About

Already performing in front of family and relatives at the age of three, Jake Fresco knew that performing is for him. With his deep passion for musical theater, he extended his theater geek fan status to performer status and starred in Performervary hit musicals, such as The Gray Paint as Cal and The Little Master as Jeffrey Stone. These shows showcased his outstanding and convincing acting skills and strong vocals.

Jake Fresco has a Bachelor’s degree in Tsheater Studies from Hexton University. He has also won various acting awards like the Omond Award as Best Actor in a Musical for the show Jukebox Jester, Lytton Awards Show as Best Supporting Actor for the musical Romeo’s Juliet, and many others.

When he’s not performing, he likes to stay at home like the homebody that he is. He also does volunteer work in Octagon Theater Charity as an acting coach to children and youths who want to do theater work in the future.
